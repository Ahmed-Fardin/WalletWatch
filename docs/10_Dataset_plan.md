# Wallet Watch Dataset Plan

Purpose:
To create a realistic synthetic transaction dataset based on actual e-wallet usage in Southeast Asia.


## Business Questions

1. Which e-wallet has the highest number of transactions?

2. Which e-wallet has the highest transaction value?

3. Which merchants receive the most e-wallet payments?

4. Which users spend the most?

5. Which payment methods are used most frequently?

6. Which e-wallet provides the highest cashback?

7. Which e-wallet gives the highest reward points?

8. Which transactions fail the most?

9. Which merchants are most popular?

10. What are the monthly transaction trends?

## Dataset Rules

Transaction data is synthetic.

Reviews will come from real public sources.

App information will come from official company sources.

Company information will come from official websites.

Market statistics will come from official organizations such as Bank Negara Malaysia and other national payment authorities.
